About Me / 私について

Career / 経歴

Born in October 1995, raised, educated and graduated in Lomé the capital of Togo (Western Africa), I created in 2015 with 3 friends, an IT company company called Dashmake. I am now in Nagoya Institute of Technology in Japan, studying AI and Machine learning since August 2018. I got my Master's Degree in April 2021 and I am currently in my third year of PhD program in OZLAB.
